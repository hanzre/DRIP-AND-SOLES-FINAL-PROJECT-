About Us

Welcome to the official website for BSIT students and our exclusive shoe collection, *Drip and Soles* We aim to bring together the best of both worlds: empowering students with top-notch IT education and offering a carefully curated selection of fashionable, high-quality shoes. Our mission is to provide IT students with all the resources they need while helping them level up their drip with shoes that are as bold and innovative as the technology we study. At Drip and Soles, we believe education should be balanced with creativity and personal expression. Our collection isn’t just about footwear it’s about completing your look with the kind of style (or drip) that turns heads. By integrating fashion with tech-forward thinking, we offer products that walk in step with the future.
